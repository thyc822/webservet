## What Is WebserveTech, Exactly?

WebserveTech is a web hosting provider based in Turkey (Mersin) that bundles web hosting with website builder tools and — at higher tiers — professionally designed, ready-made WordPress websites. The idea is simple: instead of buying hosting and then figuring out what to do with a blank dashboard, you pick a plan and get hosting *plus* a working website template from day one.

They've been in business since at least 2021 and run on cPanel, LiteSpeed Enterprise, and JetBackup — the same infrastructure stack used by plenty of well-regarded hosts. Imunify360 handles real-time security scanning. It's not flashy, but it's solid.

WebserveTech has four hosting tiers. Here's the honest breakdown of what each one actually gives you:

**PRO Web Hosting (€4.90/mo)** — This is the entry level. You get hosting plus their own drag-and-drop website builder with 500+ pre-made templates. Good if you want to throw something together quickly and don't need WordPress. Think: portfolio, landing page, small local business.

**Business Website Hosting (€9.90/mo)** — Steps up to WordPress. You get professional, industry-neutral pre-made designs you can customize. No coding required — just swap in your logo, text, and images. This is probably the sweet spot for most small businesses.

**Advanced Website Hosting (€14.90/mo)** — Premium WordPress themes, SEO optimization plugins, more polished aesthetics. For businesses that want to look a bit more established and aren't happy with generic templates.

**Custom Website Hosting (€19.90/mo)** — This is the "you dream it, we build it" tier. Custom web design service included. You're not picking from a library of templates; you're getting something made specifically for your brand.

All four plans include: free SSL, weekly backups via JetBackup, unlimited bandwidth, unlimited email accounts, unlimited MySQL databases, LiteSpeed Enterprise servers, Imunify360 real-time protection, and a 30-day money-back guarantee. The free domain registration is highlighted on the Business, Advanced, and Custom tiers.
